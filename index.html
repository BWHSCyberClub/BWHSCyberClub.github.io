Briar Woods Cyber Club


Description

Briar Woods High School Cyber Club educates young minds on how to use Cybersecurity skills to defend and fight against a multitude of threats. Mastery of cybersecurity skills and different language skills opens up a new career path for students who want to participate in the evolving technology and an IT field, offering new pathways for the future. Cyber club allows students with similar minds to congregate and develop skills which they can use in their future careers.
